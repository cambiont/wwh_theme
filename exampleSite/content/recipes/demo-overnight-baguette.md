## Ingredients

- 235g flour
- 1/4 tsp yeast
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 180ml room-temp water

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl just until combined.
2. Cover and rest overnight (8-10 hours).
3. Shape gently on a floured surface.
4. Bake at 225C for about 25 minutes.
5. Cool slightly before slicing.
