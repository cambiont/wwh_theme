## Ingredients

- 450ml passata
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 2 tsp liquid smoke
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Add all ingredients to a saucepan.
2. Simmer gently for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Taste and adjust sweetness, acidity, and salt.
4. Cool and store in a jar in the fridge.
