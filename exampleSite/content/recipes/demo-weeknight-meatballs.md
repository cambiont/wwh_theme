## Ingredients

- 300g pork mince
- 1 tbsp miso paste
- 1 tbsp tamari
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 1 red pepper, sliced
- 2 handfuls kale

## Method

1. Mix pork, miso, and half the ginger. Roll into meatballs.
2. Brown meatballs in a pan for 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
3. Sauté pepper and kale in the same pan.
4. Stir tamari, honey, and remaining ginger into the pan for 1-2 minutes.
5. Serve everything together over rice.
